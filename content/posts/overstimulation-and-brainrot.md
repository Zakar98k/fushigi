I consume _way_ too much "internet". Not in the bandwidth sense, but in the sense that I
spend a lot of my time on sites like Youtube, Discord, and Instagram.

And it's not limited to social-media sites only. Sometimes I would spend _hours_ deathscrolling
through Pinterest, Twitter, and Reddit. I wouldn't get bored of it too, so long as I keep on feeding my
brain junk, and stimulating it with whatever content I could get my hands on.

Hours and hours -- and sometimes a full day -- would go by without me realizing it.

Now I'm a homeschooled student in an online learning program, which meant that
I had to go through all the lessons and assingments online.
