# New site?
Yup. I don't plan on keeping the old one, since it's outdated and it's hard to keep it updated with new content. My current site (this one) is built with [hugo](https://gohugo.io/), a framwork for building static sites by converting markdown files into HTML.

### Why make a new one?
While I do like the ideas and the look and feel of my previous website, I also want to move on to more a more modern approach and framework. Hugo does exactly that, and it makes it so easy to update, build, and deploy content-driven, portfolio-type websites.

### What's next?
Do expect some changes to the current site theme though. I just love theming and personalizing in general, it's what distinguishes and individualizes you from all the other stuff you can find in the internet. I suppose I'll get around to theming when I finish brushing up on my CSS skills.
